Biography

Yilong Yang was born in Quanzhou, Fujian Province on February 13, 1994. He received His B.S. degree in software engineering from Yunnan University Kunming, China, in 2016. He is currently a M.S. candidate in Software School, Xiamen University, Xiamen, China, majoring in software engineering, supervised by Professor Qingfeng Wu. His rearch interests include brain-computer interface, machine learning and deep learning.
