Biography

I am currently a PhD candidate at Vision, Learning and Control Research Group (VLC) , the School of Electronics and Computer Science, University of Southampton [Southampton, UK]. My supervisors are Dr. Sasan Mahmoodi and Dr. Srinandan Dasmahapatra. My PhD study (2019.9--2023.9) is supported by China Scholarship Council (CSC) .

Before joining University of Southampton in 2019.9, I received my Master degree from Xiamen University in 2019, and my Bachelor Degree from Yunnan University in 2016, both majored in Software Engineering.
